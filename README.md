**ISD Political Readiness Assessment Tool — Designed by DB**
This tool is designed to evaluate how "ready" an individual is to engage in political or civic leadership. It moves beyond just "knowing the law" and looks at your approach to problem-solving and community engagement.


The assessment likely tests you across several pillars of leadership, such as:

**Collaborative Problem Solving**: Do you involve experts and citizens, or do you act alone?

**Proactivity**: Are you waiting for orders, or are you adapting successful models from other regions?

**Accountability:** How much do you rely on your team versus your own initiative?

**Constituency Management**: Your first response to local issues like unemployment or waste management.
